About Us

At Prixon Restaurant, we believe dining is more than just eating – it’s about creating memories. Nestled in the heart of Bhubaneswar, Prixon blends authentic flavors, cozy ambiance, and warm hospitality. Our chefs prepare every dish with the freshest ingredients, combining traditional recipes with modern twists.

Whether you’re here for a family dinner, a romantic evening, or a celebration with friends, Prixon promises an unforgettable dining experience. From sizzling starters and hearty mains to indulgent desserts, we have something to delight every palate. Come hungry, leave happy! ❤️
